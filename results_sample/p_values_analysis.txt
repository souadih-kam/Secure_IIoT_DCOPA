Secure-IIoT-DCOPA: Statistical Significance Analysis (p-values & 95% CI)
=========================================================================

Experimental Setup:
- 30 Monte Carlo runs
- 300 nodes (20% malicious)
- 1200 rounds per run
- Protocols: Secure-IIoT-DCOPA, SECDCOPA, SecLEACH, MSCR
